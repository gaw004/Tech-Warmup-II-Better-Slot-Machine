# User Research

## Personas

### Persona 1 - Marcus (Casual Casino Player)

- **Name**: Marcus
- **Age**: 35
- **Relationship**: single
- **Occupation**: Accountant
- **Location**: San Diego, CA
- **Background**:

  Marcus visits the Barona Casino a few times a year with his friends and enjoys playing slots as a way to relieve stress from his work. He's not addictive, but he loves playing slot machine at the casino. Lately, his work has been extremely stressful and busy, and he barely has any time to do anything after work. He can't make it out to the casino, so he started playing slot machine game on his phone as a quick way to relax and scratch the itch of missing the real casino experiment. Also, he love out of space theme slot machines, and he spends a lot of time playing it.

- **Goal**:
  - He wants to have fun and feels like he's at the casino from his bed.
  - He wants the play quickly without reading many instructions of how to play the game.
  - He want to feel like his choices in the game actually make a difference of outcomes.
- **He's frustrated when**
  - Games look cheap because casino slot machines are fun and have strong visual theme.
  - Screen has too many buttons that he doesn't know what each button does.
  - Slots have no special features (no bonus games, no special effects or music).

### Persona 2 - Noah (Online Gambler)

- **Name**: Noah
- **Age**: 23
- **Relationship**: In a relationship
- **Occupation**: Student
- **Location**: Austin, Texas
- **Background**:

  Noah grew up in a wealthy suburb of Southern California and currently attends flight school part time. With his family covering his major expenses, he has more disposable income and free time than most people his age — and he's always looking for ways to fill both.
Gambling is Noah's go-to hobby. He enjoys poker, blackjack, baccarat, and slots, and gravitates toward online platforms because they're always available whenever boredom strikes. For Noah, gambling isn't about the money — it's about the rush. He wants to feel the high of a big win and stay visually engaged throughout.

**What he wants from an online gambling experience:**

- Games that feel exciting even on smaller bets — big animations, sound effects, and celebratory moments
- Mini-games and bonus rounds that break up the monotony and keep sessions feeling fresh
- Fast-paced gameplay that holds his attention
- Easy replayability - big and responsive respin button

**What frustrates him:**

- Slow or clunky interfaces that kill the momentum
- Games that feel repetitive after a few rounds
- Complicated deposit or withdrawal processes
- Boring game backgrounds and icons

**How he plays:**

- Primarily on his pc, usually in the evenings or during downtime between flight lessons
- Sessions range from 20 minutes to a couple of hours depending on how engaged he is
- Browses between multiple games rather than committing to one
- Likes to stream his sessions on discord so he can enjoy playing with his friends


## User Stories

## User Story 1 - Casual Player

As a casual player, I want to understand how to play the game immediately without reading much instructions, so that I can jump into the game and play right away. The interface should be obvious where to spin, how much I'm betting, and how much money I have left. If I win, I want a clear but satisfying visual reaction so I can feel excited that I just won.
